About Funeral Assist 24

FUNERAL ASSIST 24(FA24)IS A 24 HOUR FUNERAL HOME THAT PROVIDES PROFESSIONAL SUPPORT AND SHARES THE STRESS OF DEALING WITH DEATH. FA24 WAS FOUNDEDN OUT OF A NEED FOR FUNERAL SUPPORT MANAGED BY PEOPLE WHO CARE, SPECIALLY TRAINED CASE MANAGERS ARE EQUIPPED TO MANAGE ALL ASPECTS OF DEATH, FROM BEREAVEMENT COUNSELING FOR THE FAMILY TO ASSISTING WITH FUNERAL ARRANGEMENTS. OUR MISSION IS TO OFFER ALL FAMILIES SERVICES IN ACCORDANCE TO THEIR WISHES, THROUGH CAREFUL CONSIDERATION OF FAMILY TRADITIONS, POPULAR TRENDS, FINANCIAL RESPONSIBILTY AND EDUCATION FOR THE PUBLIC. SERVICE AND SUPPORT TO OUR FAMILIES AND OUR COMMUNITY WILL BE AT THE FOREFRONT OF ALL WE DO, COLLECTIVELY AS WELL AS INDIVIDUALLY.

We offer a variety of plans tailored to meet your unique needs, backed by professional customer support and seamless claims processing.
