Our Services

Funeral Assist 24 offers comprehensive funeral insurance and related services designed to give you peace of mind.

What We Offer

  • Funeral Cover Plans: Tailored to fit every budget with flexible options.
  • Exhumations Exhumation is the removal of human remains from a grave to either a new burial site or a crematorium
  • Cremations Cremation is a process that uses intense heat to turn the remains of a person who has died into ashes. The cremation process takes place in a specially-designed cremation chamber which holds one deceased person and exposes them to intense heat for a period of around two hours.
  • RepatriationThe return of someone to their own country.
  • Tombstones We help with tombstones as well.
  • Mortuary Facilities A mortuary is a facility, or part of a facility, used for the storage and preparation of deceased bodies.

How It Works

  1. Choose a funeral plan that suits your needs.
  2. Complete the application form online or in person.
  3. Get immediate coverage and assistance when needed.
  4. Contact our support team 24/7 for help with funeral arrangements.
  5. Experience compassionate, professional service every step of the way.
